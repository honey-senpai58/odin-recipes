Grilled Cheese

Description

My favorite lunch is grilled cheese with a twist: strawberry jam. It is a delightful fusion of sweet and savory flavors that tantalizes the taste buds with every bite. The classic grilled cheese, oozing with melted cheese between slices of golden-brown bread, forms a warm base. Adding a generous smear of strawberry jam introduces a burst of fruity sweetness, creating a harmonious balance of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of colby jack cheese
  • 2 slices of muenster cheese
  • Strawberry jam (or jelly)
  • Butter (or mayo)
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Heat pan on low heat.
      2. Yes, the sandwich will take longer to cook, but sacrificing time will prevent a burnt sandwich!
  2. Butter one side of both slices of bread. I like Texas Toast but any bread will do!
      3. Mayo will result in a crispier sandwich
  3. On one slice, layer cheese in this order: muenster, colby, muenster. Place in heated pan.
  4. On the other slice of bread, spread strawberry jam on the unbuttered side.
  5. Place bread (strawberry side down) on top of melted cheese.
  6. Cook both sides until golden-brown.
  7. Remove from pan and slice to watch the cheese ooze out!
