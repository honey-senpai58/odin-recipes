Scrambled Eggs with Hotdogs

One of my favorite things to eat in the morning is scrambled eggs with hotdogs. It is a hearty and satisfying dish that's dirt cheap! Each bite offers a delightful contrast of textures and flavors, with the tender eggs complementing the slightly smoky and salty notes of the dogs.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 Hotdogs
  • Milk or cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Accent (monosodium glutamate)

Steps

  1. Slice hotdogs into your desired shape
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs and add a splash of milk (or cream)
  3. After eggs are thoroughly whisked, add a dash of garlic powder, salt, pepper and Accent. Whisk again.
  4. Heat another pan over medium-low heat. Pan will be hot enough when a droplet of water can sizzle.
  5. Melt butter in pan. A lot of it.
  6. Pour whisked eggs and fried hotdogs until it covers the entire pan.
  7. Let the eggs set for about 30 seconds before scrambling.
  8. Continously stir until scrambled egg shaped.
  9. Remove pan from heating element when eggs still look slightly wet (they will continue to cook).
  10. Enjoy!
