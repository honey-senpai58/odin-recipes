Scrambled Eggs with Hotdogs

One of my favorite things to eat in the morning is scrambled eggs with hotdogs. It is a hearty and satisfying dish that's dirt cheap! Each bite offers a delightful contrast of textures and flavors, with the tender eggs complementing the slightly smoky and salty notes of the dogs.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 Hotdogs
  • Milk or cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Accent (monosodium glutamate)
