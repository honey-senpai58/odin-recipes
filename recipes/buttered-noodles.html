Buttered Noodles

Description

Buttered noodles are a simple yet incredibly satisfying dish for dinner. The butter imparts a luscious richness to the noodles, while also serving as the perfect canvas to highlight the delicate flavor of the pasta itself. It's an affordable main dish and goes along well as a side for any kind of meat!

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles, wide
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Accent (monosodium glutamate)
  • Parmesean cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water and cook noodles to desired firmness.
  2. Drain noodles
  3. Slap a spoonful of butter in the middle of the noodles
  4. Sprinkle a dash of all seasonings onto the butter
  5. Cover the butter pat with hot noodles for about 10 seconds
      6. This will melt the butter and keep the seasonings in one place so when you stir, the seasonings evenly cover all the noodles.
  6. Stir noodles then sprinkle parmesean cheese and enjoy!
